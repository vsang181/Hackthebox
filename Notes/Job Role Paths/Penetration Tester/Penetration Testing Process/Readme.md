# Penetration Testing Process   

In this section, you will focus on understanding how a penetration test is structured from start to finish. The aim here is to help you see penetration testing as a repeatable and well-defined process rather than a collection of random tools or attacks. As you go through these notes, you should be thinking in terms of **methodology**, **responsibility**, and **flow**.

You will explore each stage of a penetration test in isolation and then see how those stages connect during a real engagement. The material also highlights what is expected from you as a penetration tester at different points in the assessment, using practical examples to show how decisions are made in real-world scenarios.

This module also introduces the preparation that happens before any technical work begins. You will look at pre-engagement activities, such as defining scope, agreeing rules of engagement, and understanding the conditions required to legally and professionally carry out a penetration test for a client. These steps are critical, as mistakes at this stage can invalidate an entire engagement, regardless of technical findings.
