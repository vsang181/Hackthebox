# Incident Handling Process

Incident handling is a structured, cyclical process that guides a security team from the first sign of suspicious activity through to full recovery and lessons learned. The two most widely adopted frameworks are NIST SP 800-61 and the SANS Institute model. They cover the same ground but differ slightly in how they group and label the phases.
